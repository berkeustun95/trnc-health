Delete Your ADA Account

Last updated: June 2026

You can request deletion of your ADA account and all associated personal data at any time using either of the methods below. Once confirmed, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Method 1 — In-app

Recommended
  1. Open the ADA app and go to Profile.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

Your account and all personal data will be queued for deletion immediately.

Method 2 — Via email

Send an email to getadaapp@gmail.com with the subject line:

Account Deletion Request

Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account credentials (email and password).
  • Your profile data — name, phone number, nationality, and language preference.
  • Your booking history and any questions submitted to facilities.
  • Your push notification token.
  • Any reviews or ratings you have left.

Anonymised aggregate statistics that cannot identify you may be retained.

Questions?

Contact us at getadaapp@gmail.com.
