Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ADA ("we", "our", or "us") helps newcomers to North Cyprus find and reach trusted pharmacies, clinics, hospitals, and dentists. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data — email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth; passwords are hashed and never readable by us).
  • Profile data — full name, phone number, nationality, and preferred language, which you provide voluntarily.
  • Location data — your device location, used only in-session to sort nearby facilities. We do not store your location history.
  • Push notification token — a device token used to deliver appointment confirmations and relevant health alerts. Stored in your profile record.
  • Booking & interaction data — appointment requests, questions you submit to facilities, and ratings/reviews you leave.
  • Usage data — basic app usage logs retained by Supabase infrastructure for security and debugging purposes.

2. How we use your data

  • To display nearby health facilities relevant to your location.
  • To process and manage appointment requests between you and a provider.
  • To send you push notifications about your bookings or duty pharmacy updates.
  • To identify you across sessions via your account.
  • To improve app reliability and debug issues.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to any third party.

3. Data sharing

We share data only in the following limited cases:

  • Supabase — our database, authentication, and storage provider. Data is stored on Supabase-managed infrastructure (AWS EU region). See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Expo / FCM / APNs — push notification delivery services used to send booking confirmations to your device.
  • Health facility providers — when you submit a booking or question, the relevant provider can see your name and the content of your request. They cannot see your full profile or other interactions.

4. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data via your in-app profile.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time via your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at getadaapp@gmail.com.

6. Children

ADA is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies, ensuring users can only access their own data. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will notify you via the app. The "Last updated" date above always reflects the current version.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at getadaapp@gmail.com.
