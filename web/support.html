Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open ADA, tap "Get Started", and sign up with your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How do I find services and places near me?

Allow location access when prompted. ADA will show relevant places near your current location — such as pharmacies and clinics — on the map and in the list.

How do I contact a facility or provider?

Tap any listing to see its details and contact options. ADA connects you directly with the provider so you can reach out to them. ADA doesn't confirm or guarantee any booking — any arrangement is made directly between you and the provider.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email us at getadaapp@gmail.com with your registered email address and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

The app isn't loading or crashing — what should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall from the App Store or Play Store. Still not working? Email us at getadaapp@gmail.com and describe what happened.
