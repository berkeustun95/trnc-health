Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open ADA, tap "Get Started", and sign up with your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How do I find a pharmacy or clinic near me?

Allow location access when prompted. ADA will automatically show health facilities near your current location on the map and list.

How do I book an appointment?

Tap any facility, select "Book Appointment", choose your preferred date and time, and confirm. You'll receive a notification when the provider accepts.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email us at getadaapp@gmail.com with your registered email address and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Is my health data shared with anyone?

No. Your quiz answers and health information are used only to generate personalized recommendations within the app. They are never sold or shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

The app isn't loading or crashing — what should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall from the App Store or Play Store. Still not working? Email us at getadaapp@gmail.com and describe what happened.
