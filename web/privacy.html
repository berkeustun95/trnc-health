ADA Health Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

ADA Health is operated by Berke Ustun. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used when you use the ADA mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Why we collect it Stored?
Email address Account creation and login Yes
Location Show nearby pharmacies, clinics, hospitals, and dentists No — used in-session only
Profile photo Optional profile picture you choose to upload Yes
Health quiz answers Generate personalized supplement and wellness recommendations Yes
Appointment data Display bookings and send reminders Yes

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show health facilities near your location
  • To generate personalized recommendations from your quiz answers
  • To confirm and manage your appointments
  • To send appointment reminders and service updates

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to any third party.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud infrastructure provider. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted by row-level security — only you can access your own personal data.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage
  • Expo / EAS — app infrastructure and over-the-air updates
  • Google Maps — map display within the app

No health data or personally identifiable information is shared with these providers beyond what is technically necessary to operate the app.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days. Anonymized, non-identifiable data may be retained for service improvement.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us at getadaapp@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

ADA is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
